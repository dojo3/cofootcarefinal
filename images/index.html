About Kirsten

Providing Holistic Foot Care with a Healing Touch

Kirsten Antony is a Registered Nurse who has specialized in long-term care since 1994. With experience as a supervisor, charge nurse, and wound care nurse, Kirsten has a passion for caring for the elderly and those with disabilities.

In 2001, Kirsten founded Heel Your Feet, offering mobile Foot Care, Reflexology, Healing Touch, and Sound Healing services. As a holistic healing practitioner, she believes in a balanced blend of allopathic and complementary medicine to provide comprehensive care for her clients.

Kirsten's skills as a Registered Nurse, licensed in Colorado, are enhanced by her certifications in Reflexology, Healing Touch, Quantum Field Healing, Sound Healing, Laughter Yoga, and Herbalism. She combines her entrepreneurial spirit with her love of complementary medicine to bring healing to your doorstep.

Areas of Expertise

Comprehensive Foot Care Services

As a Registered Nurse with advanced training in foot care, Kirsten Antony, RN, offers a comprehensive range of services designed to maintain and improve foot health. With her specialized knowledge and skills, she is well-equipped to handle a variety of foot-related concerns, from routine nail care to more complex conditions.

Diabetic Foot Care

Kirsten provides specialized foot care for patients with diabetes, who are at higher risk for foot complications. This includes thorough assessments, nail and skin care, and patient education to prevent problems.

Thick Nail Care

For patients with thickened, difficult to trim nails, Kirsten uses specialized tools and techniques to safely reduce nail thickness and restore the nail to a more manageable condition. This helps relieve pain and prevent further problems.

Callus & Corn Reduction

Calluses and corns can cause significant pain and difficulty walking if allowed to become too thick. Kirsten carefully files these areas to relieve pressure and discomfort. She also provides advice on preventing recurrence.

Reflexology & Massage

In addition to nail and skin care, Kirsten is also trained in reflexology and offers soothing foot and lower leg massage. These techniques help improve circulation, reduce pain and stiffness, and promote relaxation.

About Kirsten
