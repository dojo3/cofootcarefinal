About Hi, I'm Kirsten

Providing Holistic Foot Care and Healing Services

As a Registered Nurse with advanced training in foot care, I provide gentle, comprehensive treatments that support mobility, comfort, and overall wellbeing for clients throughout Colorado.

As featured in the Highlands Ranch Herald, April 2016

Dedicated Mobile Foot Care

I founded Heel Your Feet in 2001 after years of serving as a supervisor, charge nurse, and wound care nurse in long-term care communities. Today I travel to homes, assisted living centers, and senior communities across the Denver metro area to provide in-home medical pedicures, diabetic foot care, and preventative education. From carefully trimming nails and easing ingrown toenails to relieving calluses with aromatherapy, my goal is to keep every client comfortable, confident, and moving with ease.

Many of my clients are unable to reach their feet or prefer the comfort of receiving care at home. I love creating a calm experience that blends clinical expertise with holistic modalities so every visit feels restorative.

Life Outside Appointments

When I am not with clients, you can find me exploring the Colorado outdoors with my family, friends, and three energetic dogs. Running, hiking, and biking keep me grounded and inspired to bring positive energy to every appointment.

Why Holistic Healing Matters

Natural, complementary therapies have always been a passion of mine. I integrate reflexology, healing touch, and herbal knowledge into my nursing practice to support the whole person—mind, body, and spirit. Educating clients about simple, holistic tools they can use between visits is one of my favorite parts of this work.

Credentials & Specialized Training

I am licensed as a Registered Nurse in the State of Colorado and continually invest in advanced training to better serve my clients. My certifications and studies include:

  • Medical University of South Carolina Foot & Nail Care Course (Level II practice since 2001)
  • Certified Reflexologist through the Colorado School of Healing Arts
  • Healing Touch, Quantum Field Healing, and Sound Healing practitioner
  • Laughter Yoga leader and graduate of The Science and Art of Herbalism

Blending clinical experience with holistic techniques allows me to customize every visit and deliver compassionate care tailored to each person’s health goals.
