About Hi, I'm Kirsten

Providing Holistic Foot Care and Healing Services

As a Registered Nurse with advanced training in foot care, Kirsten Antony, RN, offers a comprehensive range of services designed to maintain and improve foot health. With her specialized knowledge and skills, she is well-equipped to handle a variety of foot-related concerns, from routine nail care to more complex conditions.

My Business

I've been a registered nurse for 22 years and founded Heel Your Feet — a mobile health care service caring for seniors and those that are disabled — in 2001. I work throughout the Metro Denver area in-home and offer clinics at many locations. I provide service for those that are homebound or prefer someone come to their home. A medical pedicure is requested most because as people age, they can't easily reach their feet. I work on their toenails, ingrown nails and calluses with aromatherapy. I trim their nails, take care of any topical issues and then give them a massage. Foot care is often neglected in elders and the disabled population and my services help keep my clients mobile and their feet in good shape. I have a passion for holistic health care and like to educate my clients on natural healing as well as allopathic means.

What I Do in My Free Time

I enjoy the great outdoors and like to run, hike and bike all around Colorado. I spend time with my family and friends and my three dogs.

My Inspiration

I love helping others discover natural ways of healing. I have studied many complementary healing practices including reflexology, healing touch and herbal medicine and I enjoy incorporating my knowledge into my practice.

About Kirsten Antony, RN

Kirsten Antony is a Registered Nurse and has specialized in long-term care since 1994. She has worked as a supervisor, charge nurse and wound care nurse with experience in caring for the elderly and those with disabilities.

In 2001, Kirsten started her business, Heel Your Feet, offering Foot Care, Reflexology and Healing Touch. Since that time, Kirsten has expanded her services to also include Sound Healing. She loves to offer her holistic viewpoints on any health care matter. As a holistic healing practitioner, she believes in a balanced blend of allopathic and complementary medicine. Her skills as a Registered Nurse, licensed in the State of Colorado, are enhanced by her training and certifications in many areas of the Healing Arts.

Kirsten holds certifications in Reflexology, Healing Touch, Quantum Field Healing, Sound Healing, Laughter Yoga. She most recently obtained a Certificate of Herbalism through The Science and Art of Herbalism.

Kirsten combines her entrepreneurial spirit with her love of complementary medicine to offer mobile foot care/medical pedicure services and holistic healing modalities.
